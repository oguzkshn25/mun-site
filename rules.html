Rules of Procedure

The Rules of Procedure govern the flow of debate and ensure an organized, respectful and efficient committee session.

General Rules

  • English is the official and working language of the conference.
  • Delegates must address the Chair and the committee formally.
  • Respect, diplomacy and cooperation are mandatory at all times.

Points

  • Point of Personal Privilege: Discomfort affecting a delegate.
  • Point of Order: Incorrect parliamentary procedure.
  • Point of Inquiry: Question regarding procedure.

Motions

  • Motion to Open Debate
  • Motion for a Moderated Caucus
  • Motion for an Unmoderated Caucus
  • Motion to Close Debate

Voting Procedure

Voting is conducted by placards unless otherwise stated. Substantive votes require a simple majority unless specified.
